# src/images/

Place your photographs here. They will be served at `/images/<name>` by nginx.

Recommended filenames (referenced as placeholders in HTML — replace `ph(...)` calls with `<img>` once available):

- `hero-main.jpg` — Match au crépuscule, Horgen
- `about-01.jpg`, `about-02.jpg`, `about-03.jpg` — Vie du club
- `gal-01.jpg` … `gal-08.jpg` — Galerie page Jokari
- `history-biarritz.jpg` — Archive 1953
- `bureau.jpg` — Photo du bureau pour Contact
- `partner-bayonne.jpg` — Atelier Bayonne
- `prod-01.jpg` … `prod-06.jpg` — Boutique
- `evt-open-zh.jpg`, `evt-biarritz-cup.jpg`, `evt-lac-classique.jpg`, `evt-demo-day.jpg`

Optimise to ~1600 px wide, JPEG q80, ou WebP.
